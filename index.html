    ABOUT SAE BAJA

    Journey


    Team TWRAN consists of 25 members divided among seven sub-teams, working as a single unit to design, analyze, manufacture and market an ATV.... TWRAN was formed in 2012 when a group of budding engineers wanted to venture into the world of automobiles. This dream became a reality in 2014 when TWRAN became the only team to qualify from the North East to participate in this prestigious event. The hardwork and vision of our seniors send adrenaline rush to our veins to strive higher. We would like to acknowledge the help we received from our professors who are expert in fields starting from IC engines to CAD to make our mission successful. Our faculty advisors also supervise our work so that we don’t face any troubles in completing our vehicle within the given time frame.

    AUTOMOBILE CLUB – Emergence

    The success of TWRAN gave birth to the club all automobile enthusiasts were waiting for- The Automobile Club of NITS. This club was established in 2019 with SAE BAJA ...and few Tecnoesis events under its banner.The club became a necessity after the amount of students interested in automobiles rose. Under this club students got to witness the wonderful Auto Expo displaying various cars from top brands like Audi during Tecnoesis 2019 and 2020. The Auto Expo was one of the most attention grabbing events of Tecnoesis where students of different colleges and schools were seen posing infront of these cars which are otherwise not much seen on our roads. Tecnoesis witnessed other different events organised under this club. The Auto Quiz where participants faced various mind boggling questions related to automobiles. Another event which saw great participation from students was the cycling event- Assemble and run. Apart from these, workshops are also held to impart knowledge about the mechanism and working of vehicles. One such workshop was the ATV workshop held during the month of November 2019, where students were introduced to a software called Creo used for Computer Aided Designing (CAD).
